Senior Design Voting Platform

Intuitive and efficient electronic scoring system to streamline the scoring process for both the judges and the administrator

Edit Questions

Our system allows the administrator to add, remove or edit questions on the scoring rubric. The criteria for evaluation can change year to year, so this is a very necessaray feature. Our interface makes this task very easy and intuitive.

View Sessions

Judges are able to view the sessions that they are responsible for scoring on their dashboard. They will also see a concise breakdown of each team they will be listening to along with basic information about the project.

Input Scores

The judges will be able to input their scores through our system. Our intuitive form allows judges to quickly fill out the numberical score in a dropdown menu while providing additional feedback and comments to each section.

Email Scores
